Ontario Makers and Mentors Innovation Conference 2017

Theme: Diversity and Innovation

This year we will be exploring diversity in the type of tools, skills, and people who make up our Makers and Mentors. We will be examining the innovative solutions that powering our world. There will be three streams, makers, mentors, and skill building so that you can explore different aspects of the theme. Makers will explore diversity and innovation through tinkering and building. While Mentors will explain how this topic applies to those who educated or mentor. The skill building workshops will offer different ways to improve your knowledge.
